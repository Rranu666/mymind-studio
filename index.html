Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

How is MyMind Studio different from Wix, Webflow, or Bubble?+
Those platforms are built to keep you subscribed — their incentive is retention, not your success. We build custom solutions you fully own. No monthly platform fee to keep your product alive, no lock-in, no "almost works." And crucially, we start every project with a conversation to understand what you actually need — not a template to fill in.
What exactly happens in the free consultation?+
It's a 30-minute call where we listen — no sales pitch. You tell us your vision, what you've tried before, and what's not working. Within 24 hours, we send you a complete roadmap: what we'll build, in what order, with what tech, the realistic timeline, and the exact cost. You're under zero obligation to proceed.
How long will my project take?+
Websites typically go live in 2–4 weeks. Custom apps take 6–16 weeks depending on complexity. The exact timeline is agreed in your roadmap call before any work starts — and we hold ourselves to it with milestone-based delivery.
Do I own the code after the project?+
Absolutely — 100%. You receive the full source code, complete documentation, and can host it wherever you choose. There is no ongoing fee to keep your own product running. What we build is yours.
What kind of go-to-market support do you provide?+
We don't just hand over files and disappear. We help you with deployment, hosting setup, SEO foundation, first user acquisition strategy, social media presence, and any marketing integrations your product needs. You get a launch partner, not just a development vendor.
What if I'm not sure what I need yet?+
That's exactly what the free consultation is for. Many of our best clients come to us with an idea and uncertainty — that's fine. We help you clarify your vision, identify what's actually needed vs. what's nice-to-have, and give you an honest assessment of what will deliver the best ROI for your budget.
What if I'm not happy with the result?+
We build in milestone sign-offs throughout every project — you review and approve each phase before we move to the next. This means there are no surprises at the end. If something isn't right at a milestone, we fix it before proceeding. We've never had a project end with an unhappy client because the process prevents that from ever reaching the final stage. Your satisfaction is built into how we work, not just promised at the end.
How do I know you won't disappear after taking payment?+
We use phased milestone-based payments — you only pay for each phase once it's delivered and you're satisfied. You'll never be in a position where we've been paid in full but haven't delivered. We also show you the team you'll work with, our LinkedIn profiles are real, and our client references are contactable. We've been building products for clients in USA, UK, and Australia for years — ghosting would destroy everything we've built. The incentive structure is designed so we succeed only when you succeed.
Can I see your code quality before committing?+
Absolutely. We're happy to share GitHub repositories from past projects (with client permission), do a technical walkthrough of how we architect systems, or discuss our code standards in the consultation call. Our senior developers follow industry best practices: clean architecture, comprehensive comments, Git version control, automated testing, and full documentation handover. You're not just buying a product — you're buying code you can maintain, extend, and hand to any developer in the future.
What happens if your company closes down?+
Since you receive 100% of the source code after every milestone, your product is never dependent on us continuing to exist. The code is yours — hosted on your own infrastructure, in your own GitHub repo, deployable by any developer. Even in the unlikely scenario that MyMind Studio ceased to exist tomorrow, your product would keep running and any developer could continue working on it. This is by design — we believe the agency-client relationship should never create dependency.
Do you work with clients outside India? Are there timezone issues?+
Most of our clients are in the USA, UK, Australia, and Canada — and we've never had a single timezone complaint. We schedule calls at times that work for you, use async communication tools like Slack and Loom for day-to-day updates, and provide same-day responses during your working hours. The timezone difference often works in your favour: you send feedback at 6pm, and it's already implemented when you wake up.
