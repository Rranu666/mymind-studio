Everything You
Need to Know

How is MyMind Studio different from Wix, Webflow, or Bubble?+
Those platforms are built to keep you subscribed — their incentive is retention, not your success. We build custom solutions you fully own. No monthly platform fee to keep your product alive, no lock-in, no "almost works." And crucially, we start every project with a conversation to understand what you actually need — not a template to fill in.
What exactly happens in the free consultation?+
It's a 30-minute call where we listen — no sales pitch. You tell us your vision, what you've tried before, and what's not working. Within 24 hours, we send you a complete roadmap: what we'll build, in what order, with what tech, the realistic timeline, and the exact cost. You're under zero obligation to proceed.
How long will my project take?+
Websites typically go live in 2–4 weeks. Custom apps take 6–16 weeks depending on complexity. The exact timeline is agreed in your roadmap call before we start — you'll always know exactly when your product ships.
Do I own the code after the project?+
Absolutely — 100%. You receive the full source code, complete documentation, and can host it wherever you want. No platform lock-in, no ongoing fees to keep your product alive.
What kind of go-to-market support do you provide?+
We don't just hand over files and disappear. We help you with deployment, hosting setup, SEO foundations, initial marketing strategy, and getting your first users. We stay with you through launch and beyond.
What if I'm not sure what I need yet?+
That's exactly what the free consultation is for. Many of our best clients come to us with an idea and no technical clarity. We'll help you scope it, refine it, and create a clear plan.
What if I'm not happy with the result?+
We build in milestone sign-offs throughout every project — you review and approve each phase before we move to the next. If something doesn't match your vision, we iterate until it does. We also offer an on-time delivery guarantee or your money back.
How do I know you won't disappear after taking payment?+
We use phased milestone-based payments — you only pay for each phase once it's delivered and you're happy with it. Plus, you own 100% of the code from the very first milestone, so your product never depends on us.
Do you work with clients outside India? Are there timezone issues?+
Most of our clients are in the USA, UK, Australia, and Canada — and we've never had a single timezone issue. We're available during your business hours and use async tools (Slack, Notion, Loom) so collaboration is seamless regardless of where you are.
What happens if your company closes down?+
Since you receive 100% of the source code after every milestone, your product is never dependent on us. You're in complete control — you could take the code to any developer in the world and continue without us.
